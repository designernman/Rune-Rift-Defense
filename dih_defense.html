About DIH Defense

DIH Defense is a solo‑developed tower defense game inspired by Bloons‑style gameplay, with multiple maps, global upgrades, and in‑match tower upgrades.

This game was developed by an individual creator. Your feedback genuinely helps shape the future of the game. If you have ideas, suggestions, or things you’d love to see added, feel free to email: atbankson@gmail.com.

Thanks for playing and supporting the project.

Help & How to Play

Here’s how DIH Defense works, including the two‑currency system and upgrade structure.

Currencies

  • 💵 In‑Map Currency (Money): Earned by defeating enemies during a run. Used to place towers, upgrade towers (up to 8 times each), and buy/upgrade the Wooden Money Hut.
  • ⭐ Upgrade Currency (Meta Currency): Earned by surviving 50 waves (currently $150 per 50 waves). Used outside the match to buy permanent global upgrades that boost tower stats across all runs.

Global Upgrades

  • Global upgrades now cost 2.5× more than the base design.
  • Each global upgrade level gives a 2× stat boost (damage, range, fire rate, etc.).
  • Global upgrades apply to all runs and stack multiplicatively.

In‑Match Tower Upgrades

  • Each tower can be upgraded up to 8 times during a run.
  • Each upgrade increases stats by roughly +20% and costs more than the last.
  • These upgrades reset every game and stack on top of global upgrades.

Wooden Money Hut (Bank Tower)

  • Costs $300 to place.
  • Generates $100 at the end of every wave.
  • Can be upgraded only inside the match (up to level 5), increasing income by +100 per level.
  • Must be repurchased every game and is not affected by global upgrades.

Core Rules

  • Survive waves of enemies that get stronger over time.
  • Boss enemies appear every 15 waves.
  • Some maps have obstacles where towers cannot be placed.
  • Use tower placement, range, and upgrades to prevent enemies from reaching the end of the path.
