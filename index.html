Game Overview

Rune Rift Defense is a Bloons‑style tower defense game featuring multiple maps, global upgrades, in‑match tower upgrades, and scaling difficulty with boss waves every 15 rounds.

Currencies
  • In‑Map Currency (💵 Money): Earned by defeating enemies. Used during a run to place towers, upgrade towers (up to 8 times), and buy/upgrade the Wooden Money Hut.
  • Upgrade Currency (⭐ Meta Currency): Earned by surviving 50 waves (currently $150 per 50 waves). Used outside the match to buy powerful global upgrades that permanently boost tower stats.
Core Rules
  • Survive waves of enemies that get stronger over time.
  • Boss enemies appear every 15 waves.
  • Place towers strategically—some maps have obstacles where towers cannot be placed.
  • Use in‑match upgrades to power up individual towers (max 8 upgrades each).
  • Use global upgrades to permanently boost tower families across all runs.
