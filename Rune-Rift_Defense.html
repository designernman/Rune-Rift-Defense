About Rune Rift Defense

Rune Rift Defense is a solo‑developed dark‑fantasy tower defense game where you defend unstable rift paths using towers, global upgrades, and rune‑powered enemies.

The game is built to feel like a classic lane‑based tower defense, but with a focus on long‑term progression through upgrade currency and global buffs.

This project is created by an individual developer. Feedback, ideas, or bug reports: atbankson@gmail.com.

Version Info

  • Current Version: v0.4 – Dark‑Fantasy Maps & Balance Pass
  • Core Features: 3 maps, rune enemy types, global upgrades, in‑match upgrades, boss waves, Wooden Money Hut, stats, and weekly update plan.
  • Focus: Visual polish, readability, and progression pacing.

How to Play

Quick overview of how Rune Rift Defense works, including enemies, towers, currencies, and controls.

Basic Flow

  • From the Home screen, click Play, choose a map, then press Start.
  • Open the Towers panel, select a tower, then click on the map to place it.
  • Press Start Wave to begin. Turn on Auto to auto‑start waves.
  • Use Pause and Speed to control the pace.
  • Use Fullscreen for an immersive view.

Currencies

  • 💵 In‑Map Currency: Earned by defeating enemies. Used to place and upgrade towers.
  • ⭐ Upgrade Currency: Earned every 50 waves. Used for permanent global upgrades.

Enemy Types

  • Circle – Basic Rune: Normal HP, normal speed.
  • Triangle – Swift Rune: Low HP, high speed.
  • Square – Bulwark Rune: High HP, slow speed.
  • Diamond – Rift Boss: Very high HP, slow, dangerous.

Tower Shapes

  • Dart: Circle tower.
  • Cannon: Square tower.
  • Sniper: Triangle tower.
  • Flamethrower: Rectangle tower.
  • Railgun: Diamond tower.
  • Wooden Money Hut: Income tower.
